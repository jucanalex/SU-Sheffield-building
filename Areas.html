Suport Services

Student Union have support services that can help anybody cope with the different pressures of exams.There is an Student advice center as well , it is confedential and non-judgemental service that help you to solve your problems.(Source)

Teaching spaces

The Students' Union offers rooms that are available to book during term-time.All rooms and equipment are free of charge to Students' Union-recognised societies, clubs and Working and Representative Committees.There are different types of room that can be booked : meeting rooms,SU music suits ,event sapces , auditorium and rooms for activities and sport zone.(Source)

Our Shop

Our Shop is a Students' Union's very own local supermarket, stocking a whole range of useful stuff.The shop have a great range of offers, such as the popular breakfast and lunchtime meal deals, as well as the 3-for-2 stationery offer that runs all year round. The money made in Our Shop goes straight back into the Students' Union, helping to improve the services that SU offers! (Source)
