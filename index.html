Welcome

This is a guide about the University of Sheffield Students' Union building made for computer science students by computer science students.

What do you need to know?

Sheffield Students' Union building is the heart of student life. It's a place to meet friends, take time out, get help and advice, eat, drink and socialise.

Things to do

It has an advice centre and activities area, a job shop, two bars, three night clubs, a coffe bar and shops.

The Students' Union can help you enjoy life away from studies and home ,with more opportunities than you think.

You could get involved in sport, societies, volunteering and many other opportunities at Sheffields Student' Union are endless.

About Us

We are two students from the computer science departament at the University of Sheffield. We have created this website for the COM1008 module lectured by Prof. Dr. Emma Norling.
