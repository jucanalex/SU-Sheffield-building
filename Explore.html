A typical week of an computer science student

Here we will try to briefly describe what we do as computer science students and how we use the Students' Union building during the week

Monday

We usually hate mondays right? For CS students monday is quite a free day, we have lectures only in the late afternoon so we have the opportunity to go to Students' Union, learn, work on assignments or sleep, of course. At around 1 o'clock we eat something at Grill 'n' GO and then we continue our work. At 4 o'clock head to the lectures.
Image by Avram Nacher via Pixabay

Tuesday

Tuesday is a really busy day for computer science students, we have lectures all day with an hour's table break. Usually on the break we go and eat a salad at the View Deli. Tuesday is the day we eat healthy.
Image by Pose Muse via Pixabay

Wednesday

ROAR TIME!!! Wednesday is the day when all sports societies meet at Bar One and socialize. At this party you can meet many new people and you can learn a lot of interesting things about sport in Sheffield and about the benefits of sport in general. Even if you are not part of a sports society, it's a good idea to go and see what other students have to say.
Image by University of Sheffield Students Union via SU Website

Thursday

Usually on Thursday we start the day with a breakfast deal from the SU shop and then we go to the lectures.The SU store is very useful because you can find all sorts of useful stuff everyday not just food.

Friday

Friday night is the moment when all friends gather after a hard week.We meet and usually go to Bar One or Interval Bar Cafe where we talk about everything that happened in that week in a not so quiet atmosphere, over a glass of martini or a beer. Good music and the vast choice for drinks and food made us choose these two bars in the SU.
Image by Mihail Hukuna via Pixabay

Weekend

In the weekend we usually relax at home, but sometimes we also go to the parties organized in SU such as Pop Tarts.At this party there are usually 2 or 3 rooms each with different music, but in the same retro style.If you like dancing and retro music you have to come at least once at Pop Tarts.
Image by WikimediaImages via Pixabay

About Us

We are two students from the computer science departament at the University of Sheffield. We have created this website for the COM1008 module lectured by Prof. Dr. Emma Norling.
