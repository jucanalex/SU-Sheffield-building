Inox Dine

Inox Dine is a restaurant a lounge and a bar all in one. They have a very rich menu both in English and in international cuisine .The restaurant is open throughout the day and it is an excellent choice for a treat at lunch or a nice dinner.(Source)

View Deli

View Deli is a meat free place.Here you can find a lot of freshly made vegeterian , vegan and gluten free treats but also delicios lunches .Everything is fresh and healthy.(Source)

Grill 'n' GO

If you are a meat lover this is the perfect place for you. Here you can eat a healthy meat meal : a burrito , a hot wrap or a salad box.And the best part is that all the food is cooked fresh right in front of you.(Source)

New Leaf

New Leaf is a unique place because you can come in and create the perfect salad or a wrap for yourself.They have a a large selection of products and combinations you can choose from.But that is not all ,you can pre-order online so you dont waste any time .(Source)

Bar One

Bar one is the Students' Union bar with the best burgers in town.If you want to eat well and listen to good music then it is mandatory to come to Bar One.(Source)

Pearls

If you like bubble tea then this is the place for you.I honestly never tried but I will definitely do it.(Source)

Interval Bar Cafe

This is the right place if after a hard week you want to enjoy a quality wine or beer in a quiet and relaxing atmosphere.Very well-chosen music and everything around you in this bar give you a sense of peace that helps you relax.(Source)
